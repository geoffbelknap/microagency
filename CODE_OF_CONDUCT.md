# Code of Conduct

This project should be a focused, respectful place to collaborate on VM runtime
infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, technical, and respectful.
- Critique code, design, and tradeoffs rather than people.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are working in good faith.
- Keep discussions relevant to this project's scope.
- Respect security reporting boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or demeaning language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic arguments after maintainers redirect the thread.
- Public disclosure of security-sensitive details before maintainers have had a
  reasonable chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that violate
this code. Maintainers may also restrict participation for repeated or severe
violations.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner.
